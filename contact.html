About Company

Damru Enterprises has been a staple leader in the manufacturing of paint and aligned accessories within Zambia over the past 20 years. Recently acquired by new investors, Damru Enterprises has now undergone a refresh to keep up with its demand, improve on the existing quality and provide new and innovating products to the Zambian market.

One of the few and true manufactures of paint within Zambia, Damru Enterprises has been trusted supplier to the Residential, Commercial and Mining Sectors.
