About Us

Get to know us

Who We Are

Damru Enterprises has been a staple leader in the manufacturing of paint and aligned accessories within Zambia over the past 20 years. Recently acquired by new investors, Damru Enterprises has now undergone a refresh to keep up with its demand, improve on the existing quality and provide new and innovating products to the Zambian market. One of the few and true manufactures of paint within Zambia, Damru Enterprises has been trusted supplier to the Residential, Commercial and Mining Sectors.


Our Vision

At Damru Everest Paints, our vision propels us forward: we aim to emerge as Zambia’s premier paint manufacturer, distinguished by our innovation, commitment to quality, and unwavering consistency. We aspire to expand our reach beyond borders, cultivating supply chain partnerships on an international scale. Through relentless dedication to excellence, we envision ourselves as the leading force in Zambia’s paint industry, setting new standards and enriching lives with every stroke of color.


Our Mission

At Damru Everest Paints, our mission is clear: to deliver vibrant, top-quality paint and paint products throughout Zambia. We achieve this by harnessing the latest production technologies and adhering to stringent manufacturing practices, all while maintaining a small carbon footprint. Through robust distribution partnerships, we ensure accessibility to our products across the nation, guaranteeing a consistent supply to meet the needs of our valued customers. Above all, customer satisfaction remains our unwavering priority, driving us to continuously exceed expectations and brighten every corner with our colorful offerings.

Brand: Everest Paints

Discover a spectrum of quality paints and coatings under the banner of Everest Paints. Catering to various needs, our offerings span across different grades, including Contractor, Standard, and Washable. Within our range, explore versatile products such as PVA Paints, Gloss Paints, SpeedDry options, as well as an array of Varnishes, including Stoving, Insulating, and Wood variants. Dive into our selection of Epoxy Painters and experience excellence in every brushstroke.

Brand: Natures Coat

Introducing Natures Coat, where nature's hues meet unparalleled craftsmanship. Our signature product line boasts 18 exquisite shades, meticulously curated to harmonize with the natural world. Experience the Monocoat System, a revolutionary approach to wood finishing and sealing, renowned for its ultra-high quality and enduring beauty. Transform your spaces with the timeless elegance of Natures Coat, where every application is a testament to our dedication to nature-inspired perfection.

We provide best services

Our dedicated team offers personalized on-site consultations, tailored to the unique needs of each client. We provide expert guidance to ensure the proper implementation of our products, promoting longevity, minimizing waste, and enhancing aesthetic appeal.
